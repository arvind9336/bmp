    HISTORY

    B.M.P Public School is situated in Rudrapur,Bhadohi(UP).It is a private unadded coeducation primary and junior high school. It was estabished in the year 2007. It is affiliated with basic shiksha parishad. It is running by Baldev,Malau,Prabhavti seva samiti. Manager of this school is Mr. Bechu lal pal.
